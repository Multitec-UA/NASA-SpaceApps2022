Space Weather.

  • October 02, 2022
  • Solar sistemSpace Weather

The SWxSA initiative is currently in its first year (FY18) and is accepting proposals for science and applications projects that will contribute to the goals of the National Space Weather Strategy.

What is space weather? Space weather is the condition of the space environment and its effect on human activity. It is the result of a complex interaction between the Sun, the solar wind, the Earth's magnetic field, and the Earth's upper atmosphere.

Space weather can affect a variety of human activities, including:

  • Satellite operations
  • Radio communications
  • Navigation
  • Electric power systems
  • Spacecraft operations
