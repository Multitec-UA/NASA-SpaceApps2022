The Sun

The sun is a 4.5 billion-year-old star that is 93 million miles (150 million kilometers) from Earth. It has a surface temperature of 27 million degrees Fahrenheit (15 million degrees Celsius). The sun is about 1.3 million times the size of Earth and has the mass of about 333,000 Earths, making it the largest object in the solar system.

Solar sistem Heliophysics

The Science Mission Directorate Heliophysics Division

Space is not, as is often believed, completely empty; instead, we live in the extended atmosphere of an active star. This extensive, dynamic solar atmosphere surrounds the Sun, Earth, the planets, and extends far out into the solar system. NASA seeks knowledge of near-Earth space, because, when extreme, space weather can interfere with our communications, satellites and power grids.
