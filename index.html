The Sun

The sun is a 4.5 billion-year-old star that is 93 million miles (150 million kilometers) from Earth. It has a surface temperature of 27 million degrees Fahrenheit (15 million degrees Celsius). The sun is about 1.3 million times the size of Earth and has the mass of about 333,000 Earths, making it the largest object in the solar system.
